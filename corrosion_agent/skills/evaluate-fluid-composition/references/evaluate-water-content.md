1. **Measurement Analysis:**
* Identify the water concentration using the available unit: milligrams per cubic meter ($mg/m^3$) for gas or percentage ($\%$) for liquids.
* Classify the severity based on the thresholds defined in Table 11:
* **Low:** < 65 $mg/m^3$ or < 1%.
* **Medium:** 65 – 300 $mg/m^3$ or 1 – 5%.
* **High:** > 300 $mg/m^3$ or > 5%.

2. **Handling Missing Data (s/d):**
* If the water content is unknown or "s/d" is reported, assign a score of **21 points** (which corresponds to the mandatory 70% of the maximum 30 points).

3. **Risk Evaluation & Recommendations:**
* **For High Scores (> 15 pts):** Recommend an immediate audit of the dehydration unit (TEG/Molecular Sieve) and increase the frequency of internal cleaning (pigging) to prevent water settling.
* **For Medium Scores (15 pts):** Suggest a monthly sampling plan and verify the efficiency of the corrosion inhibitor in the presence of an electrolyte.
* **For Low Scores (3 pts):** Maintain routine monitoring and ensure the dew point remains within contract specifications.

4. **Integration with Operational Factors:**
* Cross-reference this result with "Fluid Velocity." If water content is high and velocity is low, issue a critical alert for localized pitting at the 6 o'clock position.


## Examples

**Example 1: High concentration in Gas Pipeline**

* **Input:** Measured water content = 350 $mg/m^3$.
* **Analysis:** According to Table 11, this exceeds the 300 $mg/m^3$ limit, resulting in a **30-point** score (Maximum Risk).
* **Recommendation:** Immediate intervention is required. Review the gas treatment plant's performance and schedule a cleaning pig run to remove accumulated free water in low points of the segment.

**Example 2: Unknown data (s/d)**

* **Input:** No chromatography or water analysis available for the segment.
* **Analysis:** Data is "s/d". Assign **21 points** per the 70% uncertainty rule.
* **Recommendation:** Prioritize a field sampling to obtain actual water content data. The current uncertainty level forces a conservative high-risk score that impacts the overall integrity index.
